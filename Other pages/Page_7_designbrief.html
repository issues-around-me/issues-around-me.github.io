My Design Brief

In my research and analysis, I discovered many things that would greatly help out the development and structure of my website, as well as the content inside it.

My website needs to be:

  • On topic (Focused on the issue my website is about)
  • Appropriate (For anyone looking to read and understand the website, i.e. both teens and adults)
  • Well-designed (to appeal to to the eye)
  • Based on accurate and trusted statistics and information (So that my claims cannot be easily refuted)
  • Organized (So that people can easily roam through it)
  • Accessible (So as not to cause a hassle in trying to open it)

Who am I trying to reach with this website?

I am trying to reach anyone with influence on the school's curriculum

What is the purpose of my website? What am I trying to achieve?

I want to raise awareness of the lack of Computer Science courses in ISL Qatar

How does my website achieve this purpose? What should be the reader's last impression?

My website will feature many things, as stated above, and should leave the reader with a strong, lasting, impression that Computer Science courses are truly one of the more important courses, and should be integrated more into ISLQ's society.
