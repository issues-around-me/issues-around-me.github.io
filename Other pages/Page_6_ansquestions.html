Research Question Answer Citation (MLA)
What are current IB subjects? Current IB subjects include:
  • Category 1 (Studies in Language and Literature)
    • Language A: Literature (in 55 languages)
    • Language A: Language and Literature (in 17 languages)
    • Literature and performance(In English)
  • Category 2 (Language Acquisition)
    • Language ab initio (Beginners)
    • Language B
  • Category 3 (Individuals and Societies)
    • Business Management
    • Economics
    • Geography
    • Global Politics
    • History
    • IT in a Global Society
    • Philosophy
    • Social and Cultural Anthropology
    • World religions
  • Category 4 (Sciences)
    • Physics
    • Chemistry
    • Biology
    • Design Technology
    • Sports, Excercise and Health Science
  • Category 5 (Mathematics)
  • Category 6 (The Arts)
    • Theater
    • Music
    • Visual Arts
    • Film Studies
    • Dance

"Diploma Program Curriculum." International Baccalaureate. IBO, n.d. Web. 6 Mar. 2016.

How much of the world's population are in IB schools?
How many of them are interested in STEM subjects?		 There are currently ~5,000 IB programs provided across the world, and specifically 2,683 Diploma Programs. I was unable to uncover any data on how much students are interested in STEM subjects, but I hope to uncover this later on, possibly in a survey. "Annual Report." IBO.org. IBO, n.d. Web. 9 Mar. 2016.
